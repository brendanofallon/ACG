Features

  • Easily edit, manipulate, and visualize large alignments
  • Create plots of root height, recombination density, and many more.
  • Import alignments directly from next-generation sequencing output
  • Save and store all results and images
  • Quickly queue and manage multiple runs

A central goal of ACG is to transform large multiple sequence alignments into informative, descriptive statistics and images. We aim to produce rich visualizations of complex genetic data with a few clicks of the mouse.
